The Foundational programme is a 12-week training course perfect for children new to basketball. Focussing on the development of fundamental basketball skills, techniques & teamwork this programme will equip and prepare participants for the next level in a fun-filled English-speaking environment.

- Single session: $300 p/person
- 12 session package: $3,000 p/person


Perks:

  • Fun-filled training environment

Programme Outline:

Week 1-4: Fundamental movements & motor skill development

Week 4-8: Fundamental basketball skills development

Week 8-12: Fundamental basketball skills development, basic gameplay concepts & teamwork
