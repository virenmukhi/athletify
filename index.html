ABOUT ATHLETIFY

In Hong Kong, thousands of athletes possess a special talent.
However this abundance in talent is far disappointed by the lack of opportunity.
We're here to provide a balance.

Athletify is a professional basketball development agency based in Hong Kong. Our diversity of programmes strive to bring out the best in athletes of every level where we nurture not only their capabilities on the court but also off the court - a vital objective at Athletify. Working closely with our partners in Hong Kong and the US, we foster a bridge between local and international opportunities so that our athletes have the chance to become their best by learning and training with the best.

Founded by a team of professionals, Athletify reinforces the notion that successful careers can be had out of doing something you love. Our company mission extends beyond providing affordable quality training services - we also seek to provide a sense of inspiration for dreamers like ourselves.

OUR STORY

Before we were partners, we were friends.

Whilst Glen & Jack were studying at their respective universities, Scotty & Emanuel were playing their 4th professional basketball season in Hong Kong under the training supervision of Lap. Meanwhile, Rasheem was just beginning to launch Formula 16:3 - a professional athletic development institution based in the US. One summer, we had the rare opportunity of coming together.

Through our combined experiences, we came to a very simple but profound realization about the basketball environment in Hong Kong:
The lack of opportunity and care for athletes was slowly but surely discouraging local talents.

We decided it was time for a change.

So we started Athletify.
